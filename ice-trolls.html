Overview

Ice trolls of the Shardwild roam between migrating glacier barges and subglacial cavern-halls warmed by geothermal vents. Though towering and formidable, they are deliberate in conflict and fiercely protective of the ley-fed icefields that sustain their ancestral memory pools.

Troll kinship is defined by the sharing of breathstone shards— crystalline memory nodes carried in throat pouches. Exchanging a shard binds two trolls to mutual defense and to the retelling of each other's life-songs during thaw festivals.

Physiology & Adaptations

Ice trolls maintain a secondary heart that pumps antifreeze serum through dermal canals, preventing frostbite even during whiteout tempests. Their tusks grow hollow chambers used to resonate clan signals across snowfields.

Regenerative Torpor

During deep winter, trolls enter a weeks-long trance beneath heated cairns. They emerge with wounds sealed by new icebone growth directed by carved sigils lining the cairn walls.

Echo Sense

Specialized sinus cavities allow them to map tunnels via low thrums. Scouts can detect intruders by reading the distortions in reflected resonance frost patterns.

Sunshard Veils

Thin crystal membranes woven into cloaks refract sunlight, preventing snow blindness and doubling as signal flags during long-range convoy missions.

Glacier Settlements

Enclaves are carved within blue-ice shelves reinforced by basalt ribs hauled from volcanic fissures. Public halls glow with captured starlight stored in rune-etched prisms, while private alcoves nestle behind curtains of woven frostvine.

Architectural Note

Polar Survey teams recorded zero collapse incidents within troll-carved vaults across thirty winters. Structural integrity is maintained by harmonic chanting that realigns microfractures each dawn, a ritual led by the enclave's ice-wardens.

Ritual & Memory

Breathstone pools serve as communal archives. Elders submerge their hands to replay ancestral deeds, mentoring youths in the etiquette of oathweaving—binding promises sung across frozen canyons to sanctify alliances.

Storykeepers engrave saga knots into their fur during the solstice Confluence. Each knot records a negotiation, victory, or penance owed to neighboring peoples.

Diplomacy & Conflict

The Hoarfrost Pact binds ice trolls with frost elves and human beacon guilds. Pact envoys maintain thawed waystations along trade glaciers, ensuring safe passage for goods in exchange for rune-etched copper needed to stabilize breathstone caches.

Hostilities persist with fire giant forges that encroach upon sacred meltwater basins. Troll warbands favor ambushes staged beneath aurora veils, shattering enemy arms with resonance pulses amplified through tusk arrays.
