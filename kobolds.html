Overview

Kobold warrens lace the Emberdeep caldera, weaving through basaltic strata warmed by dormant drake vents. Communities are organized into clutch-cooperatives that pool labor for mining, trap maintenance, and tribute management.

Each cooperative pledges service to a patron drake but retains a surprising degree of autonomy. Tithes are negotiated annually and recorded on glow-ink scrolls that can only be read under phosphorescent lichen light, limiting espionage from surface envoys.

Burrow Structure

Warrens spiral around heat wells, with living quarters positioned to capture radiant warmth while avoiding direct exposure to sulphur vents. Ventilation shafts double as message tubes for encoded pebble rattles that convey orders between levels.

Surface Apertures

Camouflaged skylights disguised as basalt outcrops admit light for lichen gardens and serve as emergency evacuation routes during lava surges.

Ember Galleries

Communal workspaces where smelters refine ore using heat-trading braziers that shuffle cinders through rotating cages, preventing flare-ups.

Dragon Vaults

Tithe chambers lined with mirrorstone tiles to dazzle drake auditors. Vault floors conceal counterweights that can seal the room if tribute terms sour.

Trapcraft & Engineering

Kobold defense doctrine prioritizes misdirection. Dummy tunnels lead intruders into echo chambers rigged with thunder pots, while concealed pressure plates trigger rolling cages that funnel foes toward negotiation platforms.

Security Brief

The Cavern Census recommends carrying resonant chimes that disrupt pebble-code transmissions, buying valuable seconds if diplomacy fails. Never tamper with lichen lanterns—they conceal flame siphons primed to flood corridors with smothering vapor.

Tribute & Trade

Tribute manifests as gemstone chimes tuned to a patron drake's favored frequencies. Kobolds supplement these with crafted automata used to sweep volcanic ash from dragon scales—a highly valued courtesy in draconic etiquette.

Trade with surface folk centers on fireglass lenses, collapsible lantern poles, and map updates of the shifting magma veins. Wartime barter includes loaned sappers who specialize in non-lethal siege sabotage.

Myth & Oathbinding

The Burrow Choir maintains epic chants chronicling the First Ember, when kobolds claim to have coaxed a star-egg into the caldera. Oaths are sealed by placing claws on heated anvilstones while reciting lineage spirals.

Once per decade, representatives ascend to the Drakespine to renew the Sapphire Accord under the gaze of their patron drake. Breaking the rhythm of the renewal chant is said to awaken the Ashwind—an ancient sentinel invoked to dissolve treacherous cooperatives.
